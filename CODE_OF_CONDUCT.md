# Code of Conduct

## Our commitment

We are committed to a respectful, inclusive, privacy-conscious, and safety-focused environment for everyone contributing to QadamCare AI.

## Expected behaviour

Contributors should:

- communicate respectfully and constructively
- welcome questions and different experience levels
- focus criticism on ideas, code, evidence, and reproducibility
- protect private and sensitive information
- disclose limitations and uncertainty honestly
- correct medical overclaims promptly
- respect dataset, software, publication, and institutional licences
- use synthetic or authorised examples in discussions and pull requests

## Unacceptable behaviour

Unacceptable behaviour includes:

- harassment, discrimination, threats, or personal attacks
- publishing another person’s private information
- sharing patient-identifiable images or records
- pressuring contributors to hide failures or inflate model performance
- knowingly presenting the prototype as clinically validated or approved
- fabricating results, citations, patient histories, or validation evidence
- bypassing safety gates to produce misleading demonstrations
- committing secrets, restricted data, or unauthorised third-party assets

## Medical and research integrity

Discussions must distinguish:

- user-entered history
- deterministic measurements
- trained-model outputs
- rule-based flags
- LLM/VLM-generated narratives
- verified clinical conclusions

Only qualified professionals using appropriate clinical methods can establish medical conclusions. Repository discussion must not provide personalised diagnosis or treatment.

## Enforcement

Report conduct, privacy, research-integrity, or safety concerns privately to `othmanayari049@gmail.com`.

The maintainer may edit or remove content, reject contributions, restrict participation, or take other reasonable action when behaviour conflicts with this code.

## Scope

This code applies in repository issues, pull requests, reviews, discussions, project communication, demonstrations, and public representation of QadamCare AI.

## Attribution

This is a project-specific code of conduct inspired by common open-source community standards. It is intentionally adapted for privacy-sensitive medical-AI research.
